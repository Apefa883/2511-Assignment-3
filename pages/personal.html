Me as a person

That list to the right (or below if you're on mobile) will offer a basic idea of my hobbies. During the summer, I often go SCUBA Diving. At the moment, I am working on my drysuit qualifications to go diving in colder waters and eventually even in winter! Although I doubt I'll make a career out of SCUBA or saturation diving, it's still a great way to spend summer days and gain practical skill making precise plans and taking care of life-saving gear.

When I'm not diving, I'll often spend time cycling across the city or jogging for exercise. When jogging, I'll drag my dog along when he's up for it. Although I haven't done much nature photography recently, I'm still looking forward to the summer to get back into it.

Modpack Dev is another good winter hobby, as it typically doesn't involve much exposure to the elements. What it does involve, however, is coding and organizational skills. Making planets and machines work correctly in games involves a substantial amount of coding knowledge and the ability to organize a staggering amount of resource folders correctly.
